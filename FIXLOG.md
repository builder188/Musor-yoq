## MUHIM
- Gemini chaqiruvi timeout/abort bo'lganda ("This operation was aborted", bizning 20s timeout) bot darhol egaga xato chiqarardi — `generate()` faqat 503/429/500 (HTTP status bor) ni o'tkinchi deb retry/fallback qilardi, abort esa `status` siz kelgani uchun `if(!retriable) throw` bilan to'g'ridan uzilardi (3 ta sog'lom zaxira model behuda turardi) → yangi `classifyGeminiError`: abort/timeout/tarmoq (status'siz) → `'fallthrough'` (shu modelni tashlab keyingi zaxiraga o'tadi), 429/5xx → `'retry'` (qisqa backoff), boshqa 4xx → `'fatal'` (darhol uzatiladi, egaga aniq kalit sababi). Per-call timeout 20s→15s (asosiy osilsa ham webhook 25s oynasida zaxirani sinashga ulguramiz). Tekshirildi: 17/17 offline test (abort→fallback, hamma abort→throw, 401→darhol throw, 503→same-model retry) (gemini.js generate/classifyGeminiError).
- Yangi xizmat kiritishda bot narxdan keyin "To'lovni qanday oladi? (naqd/karta/o'tkazma)" deb so'rardi — egasi uchun ahamiyatsiz, ortiqcha qadam edi → `paymentMethod` SERVICE_ENTRY majburiy maydonlaridan olib tashlandi (flow.js ENTRY_REQUIRED), Gemini schema/prompt'dan ham (gemini.js required, prompts.js FIELD ORDER), `Service.paymentMethod` `required:true` → `default:'naqd'` (model creation undefined bilan ham o'tadi), bot tasdiq kartalaridan 💳 satri olib tashlandi (ui.js serviceConfirmationText/entrySummaryText, agent.js serviceSummary, message.js OCR ko'rinishi). Mini App'da maydon qoladi (ko'rsatish/tahrirlash, default 'naqd').
- Transaction desync: `updateTransaction` xizmatga bog'langan income `amount`ini o'zgartirib Service.price bilan desync qilardi → bog'langan income summasini to'g'ridan bloklab "xizmat narxini tahrirlang" deb yo'naltiriladi (description/sana/kategoriya edit'i ruxsat) (financeService.js).
- OCR bitta yozuvda majburiy maydon yetishmasa, save_yes tugmasi createService throw bilan, matn "ha" esa "yetishmaydi" bilan dead-end edi (so'rash oqimiga ulanmasdi) → ikkala yo'l ham endi SERVICE_ENTRY slot-filling oqimiga ulanadi (yetishmagan maydon bittalab so'raladi, paymentMethod uchun tugma) (callbacks.js save_yes, message.js routeImageConfirmation+startServiceAskFromRecord).
